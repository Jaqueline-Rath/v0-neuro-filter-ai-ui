Privacy Policy for NeuroFilter AI

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use the NeuroFilter AI Chrome Extension. This Privacy Policy outlines how your personal information is handled when you use our extension.

1. Information We Collect

NeuroFilter AI is designed with your privacy in mind. We operate under a strict philosophy of data minimization. The extension processes website content locally on your device to provide filtering features.

  • Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, emails, or addresses.
  • Browsing Data: The extension analyzes the content of the pages you visit strictly for the purpose of filtering. This processing happens in real-time, and we do not store your browsing history on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any data processed by NeuroFilter AI is used solely for the core functionality of the extension—to analyze and filter content as per your preferences. We do not use your data for advertising, tracking, or profiling.

3. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Because the vast majority of processing happens locally on your machine, the risk of data breaches involving our servers is negligible.

4. Third-Party Services

NeuroFilter AI may use secure, specialized AI models via APIs to determine if content should be filtered. When this happens, only the necessary text snippets (stripped of personal identifiers) are transmitted securely. No data is sold or shared with third-party marketers.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@neurofilterai.com.
